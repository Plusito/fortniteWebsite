FORTNITE TRICKS

WHAT IS FORTNITE ?

Created by Epic Games, the main objective of Fortnite Battle Royale is to be the last one standing on the Battle Royale Island!

Loot, build, explore, and fight to overcome 99 other players in the match.

HOW TO GET BETTER AT FORTNITE ?

  • Find the Best Landing Spots.
  • Practice Building Techniques.
  • Aim Training.
  • Map Awareness and Positioning.
  • Communication and Teamwork.
  • Staying Calm Under Pressure.
  • Listen out for Enemy Footsteps.
  • Weapon Lodaut Strategies

HOW TO GET BETTER AIM ?

Try bumping up the sensitivity slightly, then run it with for a few games or so.

Observe your performance and adjust as necessary.

While it might take time to find the best sensitivity for you,

the drastic improvement in your performance will make the effort well worth it.
